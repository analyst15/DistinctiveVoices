Joann Yarrow

Everything is a story. Stories are the most powerful way to communicate. Stories are how screens convey their messages to an audience.

I started Distinctive Voices many years ago because I saw how the power of understanding your audience is needed in the business, tourism and medical communities. It began with coaching executives, hotel and tech industry teams, as well as developing an empathy course for medical practitioners. I translated my creative experience into practical tools for them to work with and authentically engage clients, investors, team members and those seeking medical help. My consulting focused on the "Art of Listening" and "Speaking into Someone Else's Story". I developed a plan for each organization or individual.In 2018 I moved to New York to take the position of Director of Community Engagement and Education at Syracuse Stage. (ww.syracusestage.org). It has been a blessing to direct the Backstory program there, collaborating with some of the country’s most brilliant writers whose work is currently touring nationally. I love Central New York. It is this secret hub of international opportunities. My consulting moved into the world of voice-over and animation, partnering with Lau Lapides Company in Boston offering 1:1 coaching, training workshops and classes.As technology moved the world closer together, Distinctive Voices became a part of the curriculum team and mentoring for the ADA Animation Bootcamp in Nairobi, Kenya. This unique program took the time to focus on the power of story - before diving into the technology. It is so exciting to nurture African stories from different nations of the continent. Back in Syracuse, we are collaborating with the 100 Black Men to offer an Animate Bootcamp as well as bringing Kijik Studios/Simba VFX, state-of-the-art animation, gaming and visual effects studio to New York.We are blessed to know a lot of people in the industry who believe in the work and have joined this incredible team. I love to see stories to life, connect with audiences, shift thoughts, and share some magic.
